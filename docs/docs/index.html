Game Synopsis

In an alternative Universe, where the University of York is built around a very large lake, on which you can find a number of colleges. Ships are the only manner of transport to go from college to college or building to building; some colleges may be friendly to each other, others may be hostile to each other.

You are a privateer with allegiance to one College and you sail the great Lake of York on quests and adventures, and may choose to attack ships of other Colleges, try to sack Colleges, etc. The gameplay ends when the player is defeated in some way (e.g., in combat with another privateer, or destroyed by a rival College), or when the player has achieved the gameâ€™s objective.
